Ceph OSD weights and OSD Crush weights

Published: Wed 28 September 2016
By arvimal

In misc.

Ceph use a concept of weights on the underlying disks participating in the cluster to decide how much data should go to each disks.

There are two types of weights, the first being the OSD weight and the second, OSD Crush weight.
